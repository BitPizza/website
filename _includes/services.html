Intro and service
Bitkey is a pure Bitcoin wallet and try to provide best practiced key management solution for individual Bitcoin users.

Main-Feature-1: Data Backup

For most, keys loss is a larger risk than coin theft, Bitkey is implamented with trusted data backup solutions, using your own iCloud document, which is encrypted and protected by the sandbox mechanism.

Main-Feature-2: Advanced Encrypt

Weak passwords cannot resist any brute force attacks. Bitkey uses security questions/answers enhance your password, which can add 100 bits of entropy to your password. This will effectively resist brute force attacks.

Main-Feature-3: Multisig & TimeLock

BitKey provides 2-of-2 multisig-timelock address, this is a two-factor wallet and greatly avoids single point risk. Another advantage, even if your cold key(seeds/mnemonics) lost, you can still spend coins after timelock expired.
